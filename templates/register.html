🛡️ Password Pro-Tips

A strong password is your first line of defense against brute-force attacks.

Length Matters: 12+ characters are significantly harder to crack than 8.
Avoid Patterns: Don't use "12345" or common words like "Password".
Entropy: Mix numbers, symbols, and mixed-case letters to increase complexity.
