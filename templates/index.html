What to do if you're breached?

1. Change Passwords

Immediately update the password for the affected service and any other accounts where you reuse the same password.

2. Enable 2FA

Two-factor authentication (like Google Authenticator or hardware keys) is the best way to prevent someone from accessing your accounts even if they have your password.

3. Use a Password Manager

Stop reusing passwords. Use a tool like Bitwarden or KeepassXC (popular in Kali Linux) to store unique, strong passwords for every site.
